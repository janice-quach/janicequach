i build creative, collaborative teams that experiment, innovate, and solve real needs together. what lights me up: curiosity, openness, growth, and work that actually matters.

what i believe

  • clarity over cleverness — if it requires explanation, it's not clear enough
  • structure creates speed — clear systems reduce friction and compound over time
  • build once, reuse often — solve in ways that scale, not just in ways that ship
  • intentional trade-offs — every decision optimizes something and sacrifices something. say it out loud
  • quality reflects intent — the way something is made reflects how much you care about the people using it

how i work

i prefer bringing people on the journey over just getting buy-in. when others are part of the inception of the idea, it becomes part of them — they feel invested. this creates shared ownership rather than a one-way sign-off process.

what frustrates me most is when people don't bring their own thinking to the table. if i have to steer every decision or inject every idea, it stops being a team and starts being a funnel.

i want us to riff, build, disagree, explore and come out with something better than any of us could've made alone.
