how i think

clarity over cleverness

if it needs explaining, it's not clear enough. i believe structure creates speed — clear systems reduce friction and compound over time. every decision optimises something and sacrifices something. say it out loud.


build once, reuse often. solve in ways that scale, not just in ways that ship. the way something is made reflects how much you care about the people using it.

how i lead

bring people on the journey

i prefer bringing people on the journey over just getting buy-in. when others are part of the inception of an idea, it becomes part of them — they feel invested.


i want us to riff, build, disagree, explore and come out with something better than any of us could've made alone. contribution over presence. always.

what i value

the space between awareness and action

everyone nods when you name the pattern. the hard part is what happens next. i care about closing that gap — turning frameworks into actual behaviour change, not just shared language.


what lights me up: curiosity, openness, growth, and work that makes a difference. what frustrates me: when people don't bring their own thinking to the table.
